Our Story

In existence since 1999 our coaches and fighters have vast experience in both coaching and fighting in the art of Muay Thai. Our fighters are competing regularly at home and abroad and have seen the Bridgestone now Origins gym become known as the home of champions. Origins is producing more champions at present and will be well into the future. We cater for all training needs for ages 8 upwards, whether for fitness, self defence or becoming a competitive fighter. Everyone is welcome at Origins Muay Thai.

Benefits of Muay-Thai

    Physical Benefits

    Muay-Thai workouts can greatly enhance physical fitness.

  • Muay-Thai utilizes both the aerobic and anaerobic systems. This hugely increases one's endurance.
  • When trained at a high intensity, Muay-Thai will melt away fat and leave you looking lean and healthy.
  • The Muay-Thai stance combined with various kicking techniques strengthens the leg muscles.
  • The combination of throwing punches, kicks, knees and elbows provides a total body workout and develops amazing core strength.

    • Mental Benefits

    Muay-Thai strengthens not only the body, but also the mind.

  • Hitting heavy bags and pads is a great way to release built up aggression and relieve the stress from our day.
  • Training Muay-Thai is physically demanding and requires mental toughness to be able to persevere through the rigorous sessions.
  • Exercising and being in shape is an excellent way to boost confidence. When you feel good about how you look, you feel good about yourself.
  • To be efficient in Muay-Thai, one requires discipline and consistency. Self-discipline naturally comes into play and is sharpened each time Muay-Thai is practised.
