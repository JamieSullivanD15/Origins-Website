About

In existence since 1999 our coaches and fighters have vast experience in both coaching and fighting in the art of Muay Thai. Our fighters are competing regularly at home and abroad and have seen the Bridgestone now Origins gym become known as the home of champions. Origins is producing more champions at present and will be well into the future. We cater for all training needs for ages 8 upwards, whether for fitness, self defence or becoming a competitive fighter. Everyone is welcome at Origins Muay Thai.

Benefits

    Physical

    The hard workouts associated with Muay-Thai can greatly enhance physical fitness.

  • Muay-Thai utilizes both the aerobic and anaerobic systems. This hugely increases one's cardiovascular performance.
  • When trained at a high intensity, Muay-Thai will absolutely melt away fat and leave you looking lean and healthy.
  • The Muay-Thai stance combined with various kicking techniques and the use of knees develops strength in multiple leg muscles.
  • The combination of throwing punches, kicks, knees, elbows and clinch work provides a total body workout. This combo also develops amazing core strength due to their rotational movements and the balance they require.

    • Mental

    Muay-Thai strengthens not only the body, but also the mind.

  • To be efficient in Muay-Thai or anything else in life, one requires discipline and consistency. Self-discipline naturally comes into play and is sharpened each time Muay-Thai is practised.
  • Exercising and being in shape is an excellent way to boost self-confidence. When you feel good about how you like, you feel good about yourself. Possessing self-defence knowledge also gives the confidence to handle any confrontations.
