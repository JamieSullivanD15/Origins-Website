The original birthplace of Muay-Thai in Dublin, Ireland. Formally known as the Bridgestone Gym.

About

In existence since 1999 our coaches and fighters have vast experience in both coaching and fighting in the art of Muay Thai. Our fighters are competing regularly at home and abroad and have seen the Bridgestone now Origins gym become known as the home of champions. Origins is producing more champions at present and will be well into the future. We cater for all training needs for ages 8 upwards, whether for fitness, self defence or becoming a competitive fighter. Everyone is welcome at Origins Muay Thai.
